Apples For Sale

Idared

A cross between Jonathan and Wagener. The Idared has a white flesh with a firm body, and is generally considered to be tart and juicy.

Gloster

Conical shape. Somewhat tart, ruby red colour like parent Red Delicious. Good choice for backyard gardening.

Jonagold

A cross between the crisp Golden Delicious and the blush-crimson Jonathan.

Golden Delicious

One of the most popular varieties in the world. Due to its regular size, even colour and storage qualities the fruit is widely sold commercially. Uniform light green-yellow coloration, very sweet.

Pink Lady

A cross between the Lady Williams with a Golden Delicious to combine the firm long-storing property of Lady Williams with the sweetness and lack of storage scald of Golden Delicious.

Mutsu

The Mutsu apple (also known as Crispin) was introduced in 1948. It is a cross between the Golden Delicious and Indo. First grown in Japan, it was named after the Mutsu province in Japan.

Granny Smith

Granny Smith apples have a hard, light green skin and a crisp, juicy flesh.

Second Grade Apples

On our 3.5 hectares orchard we produce about 110 tonnes of First Class apples per year.

We also offer about 20 tonnes of Second Class apples per year which can be used for cider and alcohol production.
